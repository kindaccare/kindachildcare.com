Kinda Child Care Kenya is a unique home-based Waldorf Day Care program, with a capacity of up to 30 children. We offer a safe, loving environment filled with simple handmade toys and developmentally appropriate activities, all created to enhance the young child’s innate sense of learning through imitation. Our day is a balance of structure and free play. The rhythm of our daily activities offers a warm, and well-guided first playgroup experience for your pre-school child—a wonderful transition between home and school. We will be open from Mondays to Friday from 7:00 am to 5:00 PM. Tuition and Supply Fee includes one big banana, milk-enriched porridge, healthy lunch meal and 1 snack daily plus; diapers, laundry etc. We invite you to browse our website and be in touch with us if you have any questions.
